About Me

I studied at Sheffield University, UK, gaining a Ph.D in Engineering for my work modelling the the progression of sepsis, and it's treatment with fluids and vasoactive drugs.

Since then, I have worked in a number of fields as a software developer and researcher, including orthotic design, furniture manufacturing, special effects and language training. My passion lies in bridging the gap between digital design and physical manufacturing through elegant code and robust automation pipelines.
